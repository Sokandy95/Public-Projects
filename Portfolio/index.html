About Me

Hello there! My name is Andy Chim, and I am a Full-Stack Software Engineer located in Chicago, Illinois. Growing up, I was under the illusion that software engineering was only for geniuses like Albert Einstein. Without realizing it, I started coding in my high school days, editing the HTML on Tumblr templates, and customizing them to my personality. I began to take further interest in the technology industry once I started to become more passionate with playing video games, and trying to understand the logic of the software itself.

My curiosity soon led me to Codecademy, where I first dipped my toes in an actual coding lesson. After falling in love with the challenge I gave myself, I decided to enroll in a full-stack engineering bootcamp, Coding Dojo. There, I learned many valuable concepts and tools to get me ready for the tech industry, from not only the instructors, but my peers and teammates.

On my spare time, I like to dance, and learn new things to extend my knowledge. I was a competitive hip-hop dancer for many years, competing at events such as Prelude, World of Dance, and Hip Hop International. Due to the pandemic, I am no longer dancing competitively, however, I continue to learn my foundations, and am honing my skills by dancing K-pop covers with some of my favorite people. Just like dance, I'm learning new things with software development everyday. One day, I would like to have a tech company of my own that creates tools to help the general public, and push boundaries for our next generation.

Solo Project

BudgetBuddy is a financing application where you can set a budget and practice self-awareness with your everyday spending. You can create categories of what you want to calculate your spending on, and see your total spending directly on the home screen of the application.

PROGRAM DETAILS
